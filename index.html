About

Hello! I’m Yousef.

I’m a 26-year-old game developer based in Tehran.

Since I was a child, I’ve been fascinated by computers, always curious about how apps and games work behind the scenes. That curiosity led me to study Information Technology, a decision that not only expanded my knowledge but also connected me with people who helped shape my career path.

After experimenting with different programming languages and fields, I realized my true passion lies in the game industry. Game development allows you to create your own worlds, bring objects to life, and tell interactive stories — and that’s something I find endlessly exciting.

For me, game development isn’t just a job; it’s a hobby and a lifelong passion. I believe there’s nothing better than turning what you love into your profession.
